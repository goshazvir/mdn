ABOUT MDN GROUP

We are independent investment banking advisory company. Our key focus are corporate, finance, mergers and acquisitions and strategic advisory. With 12 years of experience in the sector, we have built splendid relationships with multinational corporations, private and public companies in Europe, Asia and CIS. Our internal structure and geographic spread enables us to provide a high quality advisory service in a wide range of markets and sectors.

We operate from four offices in Dusseldorf, Hong Kong, Moscow and Kiev from which we serve a wide range of clients looking for advice and assistance on their most important strategic issues. We have in-depth knowledge of the sectors in which we operate and a comprehensive understanding of the dynamics and competitive forces of each market. We know the players and we know the trends. We understand how these factors impact our clients, strategic and institutional investors.
